About Us

Together we build hope and happiness

Flyers is a non-governmental welfare organization and registered charitable trust that works to improve the mental, intellectual, emotional, moral, social, cultural, behavioural, and spiritual well-being of those in need. According to Flyers, the secret to helping people achieve self-realization is life education. We are disseminating positive sounds that promote mental
health and human happiness.

Our Sectors

Healthier Lives, Brighter Minds, Greener Future!

We believe good health is the foundation of a happy life. Our initiatives focus on promoting mental and physical well-being through care, awareness, and support. By spreading positive practices, we aim to reduce suffering and improve the quality of life. Every step we take is towards a healthier and stronger society.

Education is the key to unlocking human potential and building a better future. At Flyers, we emphasize life education that nurtures both intellect and character. Our efforts focus on empowering individuals with knowledge, values, and confidence to grow. We work to create a society where learning leads to self-realization and success.

A safe and green environment is vital for future generations. We are committed to protecting nature, promoting sustainability, and spreading environmental awareness. Through our projects, we encourage eco-friendly habits and responsible living. Our vision is to preserve the planet while creating harmony between people and nature.

VISION

The organization’s vision is to create excellent people and live a truly joyful life that is full of humanism.

MISSION

The organization’s goal is to identify, plan, design, implement, manage, monitor, and help people have healthy social lives in harmony with one another in a safe environment.

OBJECTIVES

Because Every Life Deserves Care

The organization’s goal is to help the underprivileged get equal and genuine personal development in society, as well as equal opportunities to develop robust and long-lasting programs that will enhance their quality of life and positively impact the community’s weaker segments.
