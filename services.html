Our Services

Flyers Charitable Trust is dedicated to a variety of causes, focusing on the well-being and empowerment of our community.

Child Welfare

Our child welfare programs are designed to provide children with the resources and support they need to thrive. This includes access to education, healthcare, and a safe environment.

Women Empowerment

We are committed to empowering women by providing them with opportunities for education, skill development, and financial independence. Our programs aim to create a supportive community for women to grow and succeed.

Education

We believe that education is a fundamental right. Our educational initiatives focus on providing quality education to underprivileged children and adults, helping them to build a better future for themselves.
