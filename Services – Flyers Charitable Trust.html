Services

Child Welfare Projects

At Flyers Charitable Trust, our Child Welfare Projects are dedicated to giving every child the love, care, and opportunities they deserve. We focus on education, health, and emotional well-being while creating safe spaces that nurture confidence, creativity, and growth. By protecting children’s rights and promoting social inclusion, we strive to empower young lives and build brighter, happier futures

  • Educational Sponsorship
  • Educational Scholarship
  • SHAPE ( School Health Awareness Program and Life Education)
  • Career Development Program
  • Counselling to School Students
  • Informal Education for Dropout Children's
  • Distribution of Educational Kits
  • Free tuitions centres

Youth Enhancement Programmes

Our Youth Enhancement Programmes are designed to empower young minds with the right guidance, skills, and values. We focus on intellectual growth, life education, and leadership training to prepare youth for future challenges. Through cultural, social, and educational activities, we encourage creativity, confidence, and responsibility. These programmes help shape disciplined, motivated individuals who can contribute positively to society. At Flyers Charitable Trust, we believe today’s youth are tomorrow’s strength, and we work to unlock their true potential.

  • Skill Training Programs
  • Employment Orientation Trainings
  • Vocational Training Programs
  • Leadership Training Program

Women Empowerment Projects

Our Women Empowerment Projects focus on uplifting women by providing education, skill development, and livelihood opportunities. We encourage women to build confidence, independence, and leadership qualities. Through awareness programmes, we address social, cultural, and health challenges faced by women. These initiatives aim to create equal opportunities and promote dignity in society. At Flyers Charitable Trust, we believe empowering women means empowering families and communities for a stronger future.

  • SHG Formation
  • Skill Training Programs
  • Health Awareness Program

Old Age Group

At Flyers Charitable Trust, our Old Age Group services are dedicated to caring for the elderly with dignity and respect. We provide emotional support, healthcare guidance, and companionship to help them lead happier lives. Our initiatives focus on reducing loneliness, promoting social interaction, and ensuring their well-being. Through cultural and recreational activities, we bring joy and purpose to their everyday life. We believe every senior deserves love, care, and a peaceful living environment.

  • Medical Check-Up
  • Regular Medical Camp
  • Physiotherapy Camp
  • Distribution of New Clothes

Differently Abled Group

At Flyers Charitable Trust, we extend our care and support to the differently abled community with compassion and inclusiveness. Our services focus on education, skill development, and healthcare assistance to help them achieve independence. We create opportunities that encourage confidence, self-expression, and social participation. Through awareness programmes, we promote equality and dignity for individuals with special needs. We believe every person, regardless of ability, deserves respect, opportunity, and a fulfilling life.

  • To empower and enrich the lives of differently abled
  • To support Children in need of care and protection
  • To provide intervention and skill training
  • To provide life skills training and education program
  • To address the problems faced by the destitute
  • To empower individuals through skill training
  • To provide basic services to needy children

Community Welfare

At Flyers Charitable Trust, our Community Welfare services are designed to strengthen society through collective care and development. We work on health awareness, education support, and social upliftment programmes that benefit families and neighborhoods. Our initiatives promote unity, cultural values, and mutual respect among people. By addressing social challenges, we aim to build safer, stronger, and more supportive communities. We believe true progress begins when communities grow together with compassion and responsibility.

  • Communal Harmony Workshops/ Seminars
  • Harmony Clubs in Educational Institutions
  • Road Safety Programs
  • Aids Awareness Programs
  • Screen Addiction Awareness Programs
