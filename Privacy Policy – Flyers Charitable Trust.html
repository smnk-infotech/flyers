Privacy Policy

Flyers Charitable Trust ("we", "our", "us") is committed to safeguarding the privacy of donors, volunteers, beneficiaries and visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Details: Name, phone number, email, city when you choose to reach out or subscribe.
  • Donation Information: Amount, method, transaction identifiers (processed by secure third‑party payment providers).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, approximate geolocation, device/browser metadata via analytics.
  • Volunteering / Partnership Data: Information you submit in forms or via email to participate in programs.

2. How We Use Data

  • Process and acknowledge donations.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide program updates.
  • Improve site performance and user experience.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Legal Basis

We rely on legitimate interest (program administration and impact reporting), consent (newsletter or optional communications) and legal obligation (tax and audit records).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited sharing occurs with trusted vendors strictly for payment processing, secure hosting, analytics, compliance or mandated reporting. All vendors are expected to maintain appropriate safeguards.

5. Data Retention

Donation and statutory records are retained for the period required by applicable law. General inquiries are purged periodically when no longer needed. You may request deletion of non-statutory personal data by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry‑standard controls: HTTPS, restricted access, least privilege, monitoring of suspicious activity and periodic review of third‑party processor compliance. No method is 100% secure; residual risk is continually evaluated.

7. Your Rights

  • Access or request a copy of personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion where legally permissible.
  • Withdraw consent for optional communications.

8. Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate usage. No sensitive personal data is stored in analytics. You can opt out with browser add‑ons provided by Google.

9. Children

Our site is not directed to children under applicable digital consent ages except in the context of authorized programs. Guardians may contact us to manage a minor's information.

10. International Transfers

Data may be processed on servers outside your state due to cloud infrastructure. Safeguards and contractual controls help maintain protection standards.

11. Updates

Policy revisions will be posted here with a new effective date. Continued use signifies acceptance.
